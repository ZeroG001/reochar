Legal Status

The Real Estate One Charitable Foundation is a 501(C)(3) non-profit organization and is registered with the State of Michigan and the Internal Revenue Service.

100% of the contributions to the REOCF go directly to the four major charity partners and other non-profits on a special request basis.



  




 



  

 


These four organizations were selected by a volunteer committee of our employees because of the diversity of activities, the efficient use of funds, and their community contribution to all segments of our society.
